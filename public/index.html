About Us

Where it all started...

Flying Burgers was born from the late-night cravings of two friends obsessed with burgers, bold flavor, and fast delivery.

No shortcuts. No frozen patties. Just fire, freshness, and a mission to prove burgers don’t need fries to be legendary.

From flame-grilled beef to hand-picked buns, every bite is built with obsession and served with speed.

Since 2020, we’ve been stacking the juiciest burgers in town — and we’re just getting started.
